## Hello, World!

Hello there! I am **Vedant Patel**, a Mechanical Engineering student at UNC Charlotte, and this is my little corner of the internet. You might have landed on this page by mistake, or maybe you really meant to click on this link. Either way, welcome!
## So, who am I?

I'm a junior majoring in mechanical engineering with a ton of classes to juggle. However, let me tell you, I work just as hard on my own as I do in class.

My comfort zone is the intersection of hardware and software. It makes me happy when something physical that I made works, be it a rocket launch with all the systems working as they should or a sensor reading out the values it should be reading.

## What have I been up to?

A few things I'm proud of:

- The **Astromechanics Club** has been awesome — we're currently building a **CubeSat**, which is exactly as exciting as it sounds.
- As an **Autodesk Design and Make Ambassador**, I get to mentor other students and creators on design and manufacturing, which I love doing.

One of my favorite projects that I’ve worked on recently is creating a **Water Analyzer and Biofilter System**, where I designed and 3D-printed an enclosure using Fusion 360, created a microcontroller with turbidity and TDS sensors, and created a biofilter for testing water quality for remote locations. It was a mess, but it was fun and very educational.
